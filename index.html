IISAGE

Integration Institute: Sex, Aging, Genomics, and Epigenetics

IISAGE is an NSF-funded Biological Integration Institute aimed at understanding the functional genomic basis of differences in lifespan and aging between sexes across a wide range of animals. Currently in the design phase and funded for 12 months starting August 2020, IISAGE will bring together numerous researchers with diverse technical, theoretical, and taxonomic expertise to design a broadly scoped research program aimed at discovering universal genetic mechanisms underlying sex-differences in senescence.
